Account and Data Deletion

Users of APEX Fitness can delete their account directly inside the app.

Steps to delete your account:

  • Open the APEX Fitness app
  • Go to Profile
  • Tap "Delete Account"
  • Confirm deletion

After deletion, all user data (profile, workouts, and account information) will be permanently removed from our systems.
